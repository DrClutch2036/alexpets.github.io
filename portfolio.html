Current project

Revelation station is a desk specifically made for streamers and content creators. As content creators are in a constant change of environment to the treads of the internet, having a table that is reliable, cheap, and flexible to the user is necessary to stay ahead in the game with new technology coming out in-regards to streaming equipment (stream deck) and Audio-visual equipment for content creators (ring lights, camera etc.) the space needed on a desk can be overwhelming. Most streamers play on multiple platforms and require storage built into their desk so that they can store trinkets, remotes, gaming steering wheels and other devices required to create their content. Streamers and content creators need at least: 2 monitors (one 32” and one 24”), pc tower (largest is a ATX mother board), a 100% keyboard (having all 104-keys w/ numpad), audio interface / mixer, microphone, mouse, desk mat (900mm x 500 mm). Although this is the bare minimum one needs to start streaming current games, having the flexibility to have more equipment on the desk is necessary as not all streams have the same needs. Streams have different types of content that they create such as: music, gaming, coding, talking, model making, mini builds and many more. The problems that this table aims to solve are the wire management of all the devices, movability, longevity, and space utility (competing with Ikea’s products). Since streamers need a vast number of devices, the wires need a place to go without it making the desk look messy or cause a tripping hazard. Making the table light is ideal for single individuals or those who are unable to get help from someone as this desk is supposed to reduce the burden of disassembling and re-assembling and it not fitting like it used to (Ikea….). following the previous point, making a desk that will last will save streamers and content creators hundreds of dollars in furniture as it will last a very long time if taken care of. The reason why I state that last point is that most streamers are either working low income or rely on the donations that they receive from the viewers (which are sometimes only 50 c or 2 $ average).
